METHOD OVERVIEW - SCHEMATIC QUANTITATIVE ANALYSIS
============================================================

CHART DESCRIPTION:
This chart provides a schematic overview of the Brachistochrone method.
Shows energy transformation E(h₀) → E(h₁) and speed field v(h₀).
Illustrates the core principle of energy-speed relationship.

MATHEMATICAL RELATIONSHIPS:
----------------------------------------
Energy Range: E₀ ∈ [0.2, 2.0]
Speed Formula: v₀ = √(2αE₀ + ε)
Energy Improvement: E₁ = max(E₀ - 0.4 - 0.2sin(θ), ε)
Parameters: α = 1.0, ε = 1e-6

INTERPRETATION:
----------------------------------------
• Higher initial energy leads to higher initial speed
• Energy decreases in a controlled manner
• Speed adapts to current energy level
• The method balances speed and precision
• Shows the 'accelerate-then-refine' principle visually
