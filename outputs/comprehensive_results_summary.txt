OVERALL ANALYSIS
============================================================

BRACHISTOCHRONE PERFORMANCE SUMMARY:
- Wine Quality: Excellent (0.8450 accuracy, 0.7740 F1)
- Adult Income: Good (0.6900 accuracy, 0.5634 F1)
- CIFAR-10: Poor (0.2050 accuracy, 0.1036 F1)
- IMDB: Poor (0.5000 accuracy, 0.3333 F1)

KEY OBSERVATIONS:
1. Brachistochrone performs best on tabular data (Wine, Adult)
2. Struggles with complex image data (CIFAR-10)
3. Text classification shows mixed results (IMDB)
4. Training time is generally higher than standard optimizers
5. Hybrid variants (BrachistochroneAdam) show promise with more epochs

RECOMMENDATIONS:
- Use Brachistochrone for tabular data classification
- Consider BrachistochroneAdam for multi-epoch training
- Standard Adam optimizer often outperforms on complex datasets
- Further optimization needed for image and text data
